HOW IT WORKS?

Working stages

To achieve the highest quality of architectural visualization we follow the next stages:

Stage 1 Project Details & Proposal
Project Details & Proposal To draw up a budget we need detailed information about your project: floor plans, elevations, cross sections, schedule of materials, number of desired viewpoints and delivery time
Stage 2 Starting a project
Starting a project Once the budget is approved, we will create a basic 3D model and send you a link to review and send corrections if necessary
Stage 3 Project revisions
Project revisions After going through all details and confirmation, we will apply textures, materials and lighting and send new link to review the model
Stage 4 Project delivery & support
Project delivery & support When the final design is accepted we will create final photorealistic model and send you a link that can be shared and viewed on phone, laptop or VR headset.
