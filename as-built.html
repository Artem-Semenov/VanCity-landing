As-Built

Drawings

for commercial
or Residential projects

As-Built drawings are floor plans and elevations generated from site measurement are highly accurate and preferred over the pre-construction design floor plans because they reflect the actual condition of building at the time of measuring and not at the time of construction.

As-built
drawings are only

as useful
as they are accurate

Features

Partnering with the right company to provide measuring services and as-built drawings makes all the difference.

Get the peace of mind you need by leveraging our highly skilled team of experts for all of your as-built drawing needs.

01

Improves the renovation process
With a full history of project changes, owners can see exactly what has been built and do not have to invest heavily in learning about existing conditions

02

Accelerate subcontractor onboarding
As subcontractors are added to a project, delivering a set of as-builts helps teams get access to the right information quickly, which allows getting up to speed starting work sooner.

03

Streamlines permitting process
Oftentimes, municipalities request sets of as-built drawings in order to issue building permits

04

Assists the facilities team
With a full record of change, facility teams can meet issues faster and owners can save money down the road due to improved operations and speed

Efficient PROCESS LEADS TO GREAT RESULTS

With over 10 years of experience, we know every successful project starts with smart planning. Our As-Built Measurement Process ensures that each project is accurate, complete, and delivered on time.

Stage 1 Pricing & Proposal
Pricing & Proposal Once we’ve connected and discussed project details, we will prepare your proposal with complete timelines including site visit, drafting, and estimated delivery.
Stage 2 Scheduling & Site Visit
Scheduling & Site Visit We coordinate site measurements directly with you or your client. Time varies based on project complexity – from one hour to several weeks.
Stage 3 Drafting & Quality Check
Drafting & Quality Check We draw As-built plans precisely and accurately in AutoCAD adding required details, and thoroughly check the overall scope of work for accuracy and completeness.
Stage 4 Project delivery & support
Project delivery & support We will send the final project files (dwg, pdf and photos) via email or fileshare. We are always available to discuss any questions or additional needs throughout project life cycle.
